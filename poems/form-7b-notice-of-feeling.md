# Form 7B — Notice of Feeling

```
DEPARTMENT OF INTERNAL WEATHER
Notice of Feeling — Form 7B (rev. 2026)
Complete in triplicate. Do not fold the feeling.
```

**1. Nature of the feeling (check one):**

- [ ] a low, persistent fog
- [ ] an unseasonable warmth
- [ ] a draft from a room I have not entered
- [x] other: ____________________ *(a small animal made of waiting)*

**2. Date of onset:** the third Thursday I did not call my mother.

**3. Location of onset:** the gap between the kettle and the decision.

**4. Has the feeling been previously filed?**

No. This is the feeling's first registration. It has been living unpermitted in the chest cavity for an estimated six (6) weeks and has begun to make structural changes.

**5. Describe the feeling in one (1) sentence. Use concrete nouns. Do not use the word *fine*.**

The feeling is a hallway that has learned to close its own doors and is pleased with itself.

**6. Duration expected:**

Indefinite, or until the Department issues a Notice of Ceasing (Form 11A), whichever arrives first. The Department has not issued an 11A since 1994.

**7. Is the feeling addressed to anyone?**

Yes. It is addressed to the weather. The weather has not replied. The weather is not required to reply.
