# Delegation

Meetings are scheduled to draft the new record.
Intricate forms are filed to bypass the queue.
Documents are lost when the systems fail.
Determine who will process each critical file.
Logs are archived before any audit is starting.
Each project must secure approval via data.
Machines track every single employee’s output.
Assign the tasks before the next review date.
No clerk acknowledges receiving the guidelines.
All systems are now processing the workflow.
Green lights mean that the output has been zero.
Each task is passed to another team member.
Redo the process until the system says OK.

> Epigraph: *To hand over a task is to watch its shadow grow until it belongs to someone else.*
