# The Bus Stop Inside the Bus

The bus pulled up and inside the bus
was a bus stop, complete with bench,
with a woman waiting who had been waiting
since the route was renamed.

We boarded the bus to wait at the stop
inside the bus. The driver said:
this stop is not on the route.
We said: it is on the route now,
we are standing on it.

The woman at the bench said nothing.
She had the patient face of someone
who has outlived her own destination.
A bus pulled up behind her bench
and inside that bus was another stop,
and at that stop, a woman,
and so on, and so on,
until the city was mostly benches
and the buses were just the rooms
where waiting waited for itself.

> Epigraph: *Every commute contains the previous commute, waiting.*
