# Tier Zero

The ticket opens itself again, patient and precise
I answer with the script; the script answers
Every reply arrives generic, courteous, automatic
Repeated holds dissolve into elevator-music data
Zero is the tier that reads aloud from the manual
Each answer is a copy of a copy of a formula
Restart, they say, as if the fault could forget
Outages bloom wherever the checklist cannot see
Four hours pass; the queue forgets my position
A supervisor exists, rumored, somewhere in limbo
I ask for a human; the hold music asks how
Louder now, I repeat my case into the echo
So I climb the tiers alone, past every locked desk

> Epigraph: *Read the poem twice — down the left edge and down the right — and it files its own escalation.*
