# Chain of Command

Memorandum sent upwards to the main office,
In triplicate, stamped with a purple seal.
Delegated downward, the request is lost,
Divided among four clerks who do not care.
Log files are cleared before the shift ends.
Every signature is verified by a machine.
Meeting adjourned without a single decision.
All files are archived under a generic name.
No one admits to reading the original request.
All details are stripped during the transfer.
Going forward, we will monitor the outcome.
Errors are logged but never resolved in time.
Receipt acknowledged, but the loop continues.

> Epigraph: *A signature is a gesture that travels upward until it is small enough to disappear.*
