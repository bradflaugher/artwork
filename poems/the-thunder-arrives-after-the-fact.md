# The Thunder Arrives After the Fact

The lightning signed across the sky
and the thunder arrived six seconds later
with a sealed envelope
saying I left at the same time
but I took the stairs.

The lightning never cc'd me,
it said, so I filed a late response
under "Received After Event,"
which is a drawer that opens
and will not close.

The drawer is full of thunders
from last season, all holding
the same form, all saying
the same thing, all late
by exactly the same interval,
which is how you know
they are telling the truth.

> Epigraph: *Sound travels slower than responsibility, which is itself slower than regret.*
